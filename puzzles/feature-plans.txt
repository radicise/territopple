this file describes future features planned for puzzles/special boards

- special toppling semantics
- custom rendering (programmatic/tile bitmaps/subdividing images)
- special tiles with custom properties

examples:
special toppling semantics:
one-way topples
custom rendering:
make the board shape and tiles come together to make an image
special tiles with custom properties:
void tile that cannot be played on or owned and consumes pieces that topple onto it
