This file details the time-based achievements.


Anniversary:
    this is an evolving achievement that is awarded after an account has reached an age of one year.
    Evolution:
    - after every subsequent year, the achievement changes to reflect the additional age

Cumulative Playtime:
    this is an evolving achievement that is awarded after an account has reached a certain cumulative playtime.
    Evolution:
    - at defined cumulative playtimes, the achievement updates to reflect it

Single Playtime:
    this is an evolving achievement that is awarded for spending a certain amount of time playing a single game.
    Evolution:
    - when a higher length of time is reached, the achievement updates to reflect it
